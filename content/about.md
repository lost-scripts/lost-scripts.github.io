### <i class="fas fa-users"></i> For Final Users

This site is an automated script catalog and distribution links for *MOHO Animation Software*. Instead of hunting down old forum links or dealing with broken dependencies, you can find active, updated, and ready-to-use tools packaged with their required modules right from our main list.

*   **Clean Installs:** Every script is bundled with its necessary assets and shared utility files.
*   **Always Synced:** Distributed versions are automatically compiled and updated straight from the workshop.

<br>

### <i class="fas fa-tools"></i> For Developers

The entire project operates as a secure workspace under a **Unified Monorepo Strategy**. If you are a scripter, want to review the source of truth, or wish to replicate the automated deployment workflow, everything is open and documented in the [.github](https://github.com/lost-scripts/.github "Go to the community health repo...") repository of our organization:

*   **[Development Guide:](https://github.com/lost-scripts/.github/blob/main/DEVELOPMENT.md)** Learn about the monorepo architecture, script dependency injection (`ScriptDeps`), and how the pipeline cooks metadata.
*   **[Environment Specs:](https://github.com/lost-scripts/.github/blob/main/ENVIRONMENT.md)** Check the global environment setups, custom Git profiles, and automation tool configurations.
*   **[License Information:](https://github.com/lost-scripts/.github/blob/main/LICENSE)** Review the permissive terms, architecture restrictions, and compatibility guidelines for derivative forks.
